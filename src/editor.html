Camera types:
Fixed (default):
The camera never moves and the whole level is displayed.
By screen (flickscreen):
The world is divided in a grid of screens and only the screen that contains the player's avatar(s) is displayed.
Centered on player (zoomscreen):
The camera follows the player's avatar so that it's at the center of the fixed-size screen.
